What is YouTube Reception Mood Check?

YouTube Reception Mood Check is a free tool that lets you instantly understand how people feel about a YouTube video. Paste any video URL, and the app scrapes every accessible comment, runs each one through a multilingual AI sentiment model, and presents a clear visual breakdown of positive, negative, and neutral reactions.

How it works

  1. Scraping — We use yt-dlp to talk directly to YouTube’s internal API, capturing every top-level comment and reply that is publicly visible.
  2. Sentiment analysis — Each comment is classified by a multilingual XLM-RoBERTa transformer model trained on ~198 million tweets across eight languages, including Brazilian Portuguese and English.
  3. User corrections — Disagree with a label? Click any sentiment badge to correct it. Your feedback is saved locally and used to improve future analyses.

Tech stack

  • Back-end: Python · Flask
  • Scraping: yt-dlp (YouTube InnerTube API)
  • AI model: HuggingFace Transformers · cardiffnlp/twitter-xlm-roberta-base-sentiment-multilingual
  • Front-end: Vanilla HTML / CSS / JS
